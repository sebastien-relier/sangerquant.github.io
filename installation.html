SangerQuant

Fast Quantification of Sequence Mismatch from Sanger files

About Installation Tutorial

Installation

Windows
  • Download the .zip archive for windows
  • Right click on the downloaded archive > "Extract"
  • Open the folder "windows-executable-sangerquant" > "sangerquant" > double click on sangerquant.exe
    • MacOS
  • Download the .zip archive for macos
  • Right click on the downloaded archive > "Extract"
  • Open the folder "macos-sangerquant" > "sangerquant" > double click on sangerquant.exe
    • Linux / Ubuntu
  • Download the .zip archive for ubuntu
  • Right click on the downloaded archive > "Extract"
  • Open the linux-executable-sangerquant > sangerquant > double click on sangerquant.exe
